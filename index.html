About Application

Our app for sharing used goods provides a platform for users to easily connect and exchange items they no longer need. With our app, users can create listings for their used goods and browse through others' listings to find items they are interested in. The app also includes a messaging feature, allowing users to communicate with each other to coordinate the exchange of items. By using our app, users can not only free up space in their homes by getting rid of unwanted items, but also help reduce waste and promote sustainability by giving these items a new life. Join our community of users today and start sharing your used goods!

Feature

Local Sharing

Local Sharing is a feature in the app that allows users to share and search for pre-owned items in their local area, reducing resource waste and creating opportunities to reuse valuable items.

Connecting

Connecting is a feature that connects users during the process of sharing used items, enhancing the user experience and creating a community of valuable item sharing within the local area through chat or direct call.

Reverse Haggle

Reverse Haggle allows buyers to propose a purchase price lower than the current selling price, and sellers can either accept or reject the proposal.
