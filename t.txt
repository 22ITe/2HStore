The Reverse Haggle feature in the Used Goods Sharing app allows buyers to propose a price lower than the current selling price offered by the seller. When the buyer makes their proposal, the seller will receive a notification and can choose to accept or reject the offer.
<br> <br>
If the seller accepts the buyer's proposed price, the transaction will be completed, and the buyer will purchase the item at their proposed price. This feature helps buyers save on the cost of purchasing goods, while sellers can find buyers more quickly and sell products at reasonable prices.
<br> <br>
Reverse Haggle is a useful feature in the Used Goods Sharing app, helping users optimize their buying and selling process and exchange their goods more efficiently.
